Euclid's Postulates

  1. Any two points can be connected by a straight line
  2. Any straight line can be extended indefinitely
  3. A circle can be drawn with any center and radius
  4. All right angles are equal
  5. Through a point not on a line, exactly one parallel line can be drawn

The fifth postulate changes in non-Euclidean geometries!
